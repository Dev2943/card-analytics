Credit Card Portfolio Analytics

Behavioral segmentation, default risk, and risk-adjusted profitability across a 7,500-account held-out portfolio. Default probabilities are model-scored; expected loss follows PD × EAD × LGD.

Key finding

On raw revenue, the Stressed / At-Risk segment looks like a contributor at NT$583K/mo. Risk-adjusted, it is −NT$891K/mo — the expected losses dwarf the revenue. The most active segment is the one destroying the most portfolio value, which is why card portfolios are managed on risk-adjusted return, not revenue.
